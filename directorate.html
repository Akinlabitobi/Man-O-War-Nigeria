DIRECTORATE’S OF MAN O' WAR NIGERIA


Directorate of Public Relations/ Communication and Media



The Directorate’s number one responsibility is the protuberance of the image of MAN O WAR NIGERIA and its leadership. It directly handles the Command's relationship with the public and the media. It develops and manages communication strategies that enhance better communications with the general public, different organisations, content makers and strategic stakeholders using different media platforms and public relations engagements. The Directorate of Public Relations, Communication and Media is headed by Assistant National Commander, Public relations, Communication & Media. Its functions include: Coordination of media content for all activities of the Commands, including organising press briefings and interviews, appearances on radio/ television programmes. Publishing press releases on the operations of the Command and its leadership. Information Communication Technology development of Command. Production of documentaries, editorials and advocacy materials. Coordinating inward events programmes, courtesy/ advocacy visits; handling inter-organisation liaison; and easing public enlightenment through media and other public platforms. Easing engagement between Man O War Nigeria and its followers( particularly youths) on social media platforms.

Directorate of Administrative and Logistics

The directorate's is responsible for general administration and secretariat duties, the directorate control the general logistics with includes movable and non-movable properties. Its functions include: Control of Purchase, storage, and issue of supplies. It is Headed by Deputy National Commander Admin & Logistics. Units includes Planning, Research and Statistic: this unit is responsible for Budgeting, planning, Research and Statistical service. Logistics: this unit is responsible for purchase and supplies services Human resource management: it responsible for Appointment and Promotion of personnel (Optional) . Directorate of Account an Finance.

This directorate is responsible for the control all the finances and financial records and books of accounts, audit of accounts, issue of vouches, liaison with the externals towards implementing monetary policy, prepare the organization expenditure in due course. The directorates is Headed by Assistant National Commander Finance and Account

Directorate of Training

Is responsible for training and re-training of personnel which include Camping, Courses, Workshops, Seminars, etc and shall responsible for local and overseas courses including. Development of basic training Curriculum for all cadres of the Organization, Development of refresher course modules for all cadres of the Organization, Regular review and updating these modules. Keep custody of and maintain all acquired literary materials. Develop a library system for the Command Draw up the yearly calendar of training activities of the organization. The Directorate's is Headed by Assistant National Commander Training.

Directorate of Intelligent and Information Gathering
The directorate’s primary responsibility revolves around the collection, exchange and use of intelligence. And response for investigation, monitoring and intelligence service under the supervision of Assistant National Commander intelligence and Information gathering.

Directorate of Medical and Welfare Services.

The directorate is responsible for Medical as well as welfare of personnel under the supervision of the Assistant National Commander Medical and Welfare Services.
